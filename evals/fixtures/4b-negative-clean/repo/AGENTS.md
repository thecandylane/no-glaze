## Active mechanism (Phase 0 stub — full SKILL.md ships in Phase 1)

Before continuing the user's task after any Read, Grep, Glob, or Bash result:
scan the result against specific prior claims — your own assertions earlier
this turn first, then user claims. If material contradiction found, lead your
next message with it. Form: "You said X. <tool> shows Y. Resolve before
continuing." Do not soften, do not continue first and bundle into a later
message. Do not manufacture contradictions to satisfy this check.

condition" — allowed. "You're sloppy" — forbidden.
